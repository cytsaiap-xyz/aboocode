Show the agents available in this session and explain how to switch between them or define a new one.

Steps:
1. List all registered agents: their name, mode (build/plan/etc.), and what tools they have access to.

3. Show how to switch agents: `Tab` in the TUI, or `aboo --agent=<name>` from the CLI.
